Intro

Vita:

Syu-Siang Wang earned his Bachelor’s degree (2008) and Master’s degree (2010) in Electrical Engineering from National Changhua University of Education in Changhua, Taiwan, and National Chi Nan University in Nantou, Taiwan, respectively. In 2018, he completed his Ph.D. at the Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering, National Taiwan University. During his doctoral studies, he served as a research assistant at the Research Center for Information Technology Innovation, Academia Sinica, from 2012 to 2018, specializing in robust speech feature extraction and enhancement. Following his Ph.D., Dr. Wang pursued postdoctoral research at the NTU Joint Research Center for AI Technology and All Vista Healthcare, focusing on signal processing technologies for hearing aids. He is currently an assistant professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Yuan Ze University, Taoyuan, Taiwan. His research interests include speech signal processing and biomedical signal processing.

簡歷:

王緒翔博士分別於國立彰化師範大學電機工程學系及國立暨南國際大學電機工程研究所獲得學士學位（2008 年）和碩士學位（2010 年），並在2018年於台灣大學電信工程研究所獲得了博士學位。在學期間，王博士同時任職於中央研究院資訊科技創新研究中心擔任研究助理，研究主題為強健性語音特徵擷取與語音增強技術。於博士畢業後，王博士在臺大人工智慧研究中心擔任博士後研究員，從事助聽器信號處理技術研究。目前，王博士為元智大學電機工程學系助理教授。研究主題包括語音訊號處理和生物信號處理。
